# BANK LOAN PORTFOLIO ANALYSIS DASHBOARD | POWER BI

An end-to-end POWER BI   business intelligence solution analyzing 38,600+ loan appliactions to monitor portfolio health, detect risk, and drive data-informed lending decisions.

**Short Description**
This interactive POWER BI dashboard transforms raw bank loan data into actionable intelligence across three analytical layers- Summary, Overview, Details- enabling stakeholders to track loan performance, identity risk concentration and benchamark lending trends at a glance.

**Tech Stack**
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
• 📂 Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures, dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.
• 📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews


**Problem Statement**
Banks generate thousands of loan records monthly, but critical risk signals stay buried in raw data. Without a unified view, risk teams can't quickly gauge default exposure, management lacks regional lending breakdowns, and analysts lose hours aggregating KPIs manually — slowing decisions where speed matters most.
Key Questions such as: Are the bank lending well? Are the bank getting their money back?

**Findings:**
Total loan applications: 38.6k Total funded amount: $435.8M Total amount received back: $473.1M Average interest rate: 12.05% Average DTI: 13.33%
Good loans: 86.18% (33.2K applications, $370.2M funded, $435.8M received) Bad loans: 13.82% (5.3K applications, $65.5M funded, $37.3M received)
Patterns: Applications grew every month, from 2.3k in Jan to 4.3k in Dec Debt consolidation is the top reason for loans (18.2K applications) Most borrowers prefer 36-month terms (28.2K) over 60-month (10.3K) People with 10+ years of work experience apply the most (8.9K) Most borrowers are on Rent (18,439) or Mortgage (17,198), very few own a house

**Business Impact:**
Charged off loans are only 13.82% in count, but look closer: The bank gave $65.5M to these borrowers and got back just $37.3M. That's a real loss of $28.2M.
Revealed debt consolidation as the dominant loan purpose (18K applications)
